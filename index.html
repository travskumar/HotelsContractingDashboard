Score Formula

Contracting score is directional: 28% booking volume + 26% room nights + 16% month spread + 12% agency city spread + 10% supplier consolidation opportunity + 5% refundability opportunity + 3% hotel breadth.
⚠ Scores are relative to the current filtered dataset — not fixed benchmarks. A score of 80 after filtering to one country is not comparable to 80 in the full dataset. Higher supplier count = higher consolidation opportunity score.
